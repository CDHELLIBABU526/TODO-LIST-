About ToDo App

ToDo App is designed to help you stay on top of your tasks with ease. Whether it's organizing your work schedule, planning your day, or just remembering groceries — this app is built to keep you productive and stress-free.

Why Choose ToDo App?

We understand that staying organized shouldn't be a chore. That’s why we’ve built an interface that’s clean, intuitive, and packed with just the right features — no clutter, no distractions.

Key Features

  • Add, edit, and delete tasks
  • Mark tasks as completed
  • Organize tasks by categories and deadlines
  • Simple and minimalistic user interface

Our Mission

We believe that small habits lead to big changes. Our goal is to help you take control of your time, build consistency, and achieve more — one task at a time.

Who Is It For?

  • Students managing assignments and exams
  • Professionals juggling multiple work projects
  • Anyone who loves staying organized!
